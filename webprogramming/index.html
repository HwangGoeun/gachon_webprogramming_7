INTRODUCE

Blockchain is an algorithm that combines multiple transaction details to form a block, connects several blocks like a chain using a hash, and then copies and stores them.
Blockchain is a key technology used in cryptocurrency such as Bitcoin and Ethereum. Even if there are no third-party intermediaries such as banks, anyone can make reliable and safe transactions using blockchain technology. Blockchain can be used not only for cryptocurrency, but also for processing all data that has online transactions and requires history management. Blockchain-based smart contracts, logistics management systems, document management systems, medical information management systems, copyright management systems, social media management systems, game item management systems, electronic voting systems, and identification systems are available.

Blockchain is very broad and other concepts (software architecture, encryption, etc.) are also extensive, so please visit the site for more information!


DEVELOPE

Blockchain can be developed in various languages.

However, one of the important technologies of blockchain, Smart Contract, can be developed with Solidity. Solidity is a contract-oriented programming language used for smart contract creation and implementation on blockchain platforms such as Ethereum. Solidity was developed by key Ethereum contributors to create smart contracts on blockchain platforms such as Ethereum. Developers can implement their own business logic in smart contracts through solidity. Various apps can be implemented through solidity, but due to the nature of the blockchain, smart contracts cannot be modified once they are on the blockchain, and anyone can check them, so they should be carefully written.

NFT(Non-Fungible Token)

Non-fungible tokens (NFTs) are cryptocurrencies that cannot replace one token with another. It is abbreviated in English and is called NFT. It is similar to Cryptokitty, a blockchain cat game. In the Cryptokitty game, each cat has its own unique characteristics, making it impossible to replace it with another cat. The ERC-721 token is the implementation of an irreplaceable token on the Ethereum network. ERC-20, on the other hand, is an alternative token. For example, one Ethereum can be replaced by another Ethereum. The element that distinguishes between possible and impossible substitution depends on whether each has its own information or characteristics.
